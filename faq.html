FAQ

How early should I order?

The beginning of the month is the best time since there is a point at which we can no longer take any more orders due to being a small business.

Are there any gluten free/vegan options?

Unfortunately at this time, there is not. In the future, we will try to incorporate more inclusive options such as this.

Is there an ingredient listing as I might be allergic to something?

Yes there is! All of our pastries list the ingredients used as to make sure no one gets an allergic reaction to anything.

Can I have my pastries delivered on-site?

We are currently experimenting with this. Send us an email or check out or socials in the contact page to inqury further, but primarily they are to be picked up.

Is there any way to try a free sample?

At this current moment in time, there is not.
