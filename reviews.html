Reviews


Christina H. San Antonio, TX

My mother, back when I was visiting California, told me about Sylvia's Pastires and I just had to try it for myself. I ordered
the empanadas and was blown away with how crispy the bread was! The meat and other flavors meshed together very well.
Now when I got to Cali, I always make it a point to order when I can.

Tina C. San Jose, CA

I always order from Sylvia's Pastries when I host parties! Their sweets are so delicious, I just can't get enough!

Josie L. San Jose, CA

The empanadas are amazing. I've never tasted one that had such a good crust and a hint of sweet insides.

Robert R. San Jose, CA

I like to support small businesses around my area and I have to say, I don't regret trying out Sylvia's Pastries.

Charles M. Portland, OR

My cousin offered some of the food here while I was visiting for a birthday party. Those chocolate cookies tasted
